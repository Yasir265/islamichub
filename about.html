About Islamic Hub Academy

Empowering souls worldwide with authentic, accessible, and transformative Quranic education rooted in tradition and delivered with excellence.

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by a group of passionate Islamic scholars and educators who saw the growing need for high-quality, authentic online Quran learning.

Islamic Hub Academy began with a simple yet profound mission: to make authentic Quranic education accessible to Muslims everywhere, regardless of location, schedule, or background.

We combine centuries-old Islamic teaching traditions with modern technology, offering one-on-one live classes with certified scholars who emphasize Tajweed, understanding, and spiritual connection. Today, we proudly serve thousands of students across more than 50 countries, helping children, adults, and entire families grow closer to the Quran.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver authentic, personalized, and transformative Quranic education that nurtures spiritual growth, correct recitation with Tajweed, and deep understanding of the Holy Quran for learners of all ages, worldwide.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted global platform for online Islamic learning, raising a generation of confident Muslims who recite, understand, and live by the Quran with excellence and devotion.

Our Scholars & Teachers

All our instructors are carefully selected, highly qualified, and deeply committed to ethical Islamic teaching.

Our teaching team consists of certified Quran scholars with Ijazah in various Qira'at, graduates from renowned Islamic institutions, and years of teaching experience.

Every teacher undergoes thorough background checks, ongoing training, and evaluation to ensure the highest standards of knowledge, character, and pedagogy.

We maintain a balanced team of both male and female teachers to accommodate family preferences, with a special emphasis on patience, empathy, and creating a supportive learning environment.
